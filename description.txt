This mod is Calamity Addon of Pets Overhaul mod, which adds multiple new effects to existing Pets & integration of Calamity content to Pets Overhaul!

Weekly Updates!

Features this addon adds:
21 More Unique Pet Effects
11 More Unique Light Pet Effects
Compatibility systems to integrate Calamity content with existing systems of Pets Overhaul

Use /pet command to see all Pets Overhaul commands in-game. ("/pet" = help command)
