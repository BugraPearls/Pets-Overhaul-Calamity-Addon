[b]This mod is Calamity Addon of Pets Overhaul mod, which adds multiple new effects to existing Pets & integration of Calamity content to Pets Overhaul![/b]

[h3]Weekly Updates![/h3]

[h3]Features this addon adds[/h3]
[list]
[*] 21 More Unique Pet Effects
[*] 11 More Light Pet Effects
[*] Compatibility systems to integrate Calamity content with existing systems of Pets Overhaul
[/list]

Use /pet command to see all Pets Overhaul commands in-game. ("/pet" = help command)
